## General Details

* 8th century BCE (~ 750?)
* Ionia (western coast of Asia Minor) (*Ionic* dialect)
* **epic** genre: war, dactylic hexameter

## Works

* *Iliad*: last year of the Trojan War
* *Odyssey*: journeys after the Trojan War ends
